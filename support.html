Support

Storm Robotics

How to support us:

  • Checks made out to "Georgia Robotics Alliance - Storm Robotics" / Contact Us for mailing address
  • Wire Transfer / Contact Us for account details
  • ETransfer / @gastormrobotics on venmo

As a young, student-led community based team, Storm Robotics does not enjoy certain safeguards - especially financially, enjoyed by school-based teams. Therfore, it is up to a bunch of high-schoolers to ensure the long-term financial stability of the team. Sponsors not only help towards our mission of creating a self-sustaining team to serve as a beacon of STEM outreach in the communities of Alpharetta and Milton, but also enjoy the benefits of local, regional, and international recognition through our sucess and competitive play. Storm Robotics prides itself on being a reflection of its sponsors around the globe. Learn more about our mission and why you should support us through our sponsorship document below!

Sponsorship Document

EXPANSION: Coming back from our enriching experience at the FIRST World Championships, Storm Robotics has had the opportunity to reflect on our place in our community and our role in spreading STEM to the young students around us. As a result, we have unanimously concluded that we must seek to expand our operations to become a stable foundation and a catalyst for robotics education in Alpharetta and Milton.
