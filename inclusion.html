GirlsFIRST Grant

Learn about our latest initiative to increase the participation of females in FIRST! Apply Here

This grant is designed to remove the bulk of the financial burden on prospective female members! This grant is open to any current or prospective member who identifies as female, non-binary, trans, or any other underrepreseted gender orientation in STEM . If you are interested in supporting/sponsoring this effort, please contact us or visit our support page

MinoritiesFIRST Grant

Learn about our latest initiative to increase the participation of minorities in FIRST! Apply Here

This grant is designed to remove the bulk of the financial burden on prospective minority members! This grant is open to any current or prospective member who identifies as Underrepresented Populations: Persons of Latinx Descent Black/African Americans Native American, Indigenous Persons, Alaskan Natives Native Hawaiian, Asian, Pacific Islanders Underserved populations Vulnerable Youths. If you are interested in supporting/sponsoring this effort, please contact us or visit our support page
